About Baroda


Baroda is basically a collective name given to 2 villages :-

  • Baroda Thuthan
  • Baroda Mor


Baorda Thuthan


According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Baroda Thuthan village is 059646. Baroda Thuthan village is located in Gohana tehsil of Sonipat district in Haryana, India. It is situated 8km away from sub-district headquarter Gohana (tehsildar office) and 44km away from district headquarter Sonipat. As per 2009 stats, Baroda Tuthan is the gram panchayat of Baroda Thuthan village.

The total geographical area of village is 852 hectares. Baroda Thuthan has a total population of 4,157 peoples, out of which male population is 2,238 while female population is 1,919. Literacy rate of baroda thuthan village is 66.11% out of which 73.28% males and 57.74% females are literate. There are about 784 houses in baroda thuthan village. Pincode of baroda thuthan village locality is 131304.



Baroda Mor


According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Baroda Mor village is 059647. Baroda Mor village is located in Gohana tehsil of Sonipat district in Haryana, India. It is situated 8km away from sub-district headquarter Gohana (tehsildar office) and 46km away from district headquarter Sonipat. As per 2009 stats, Baroda Mor village is also a gram panchayat.

The total geographical area of village is 2029 hectares. Baroda Mor has a total population of 7,103 peoples, out of which male population is 3,858 while female population is 3,245. Literacy rate of baroda mor village is 64.41% out of which 70.92% males and 56.67% females are literate. There are about 1,297 houses in baroda mor village. Pincode of baroda mor village locality is 131304.
